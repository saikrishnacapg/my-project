Tips to maximise your potential savings on the PowerBank Saver Plan


Cooling and heating

  • Don't crank the air conditioner. Set it at 24°C in summer and 18°C in winter, and remember to cool or heat your house up slowly.
  • Close the curtains to keep the heat out in summer, and open them in winter to let the heat in. A door snake can also keep cold or warm air from escaping.

    • Audio and home entertainment

  • Don't leave the TV on for the sake of it. Once you or the kids lose interest, it's time to switch it off.
  • Switch your appliances off at the wall. Leaving appliances on standby mode increases energy use and costs.
