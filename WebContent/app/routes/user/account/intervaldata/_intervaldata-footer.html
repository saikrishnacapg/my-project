Load Factor - Measures how constant energy demand is over a period of time. Load factor is calculated by dividing the Average kW Demand by the Maximum kW Demand for a given period. A load factor of 1 (ie. the average and the maximum demand are equal) means that the electric system exhibits a high level of efficiency.
