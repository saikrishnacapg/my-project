Compares the difference between Midday Saver plan and Home Plan A1 for the dates selected in 'Selected Period'. The default dates are your last bill dates. These can be changed to compare a different period.

If you are saving on Midday Saver for 'Selected Period', then the savings will be displayed in green.

If you are not saving on the Midday Saver plan for 'Selected Period', then the results will be displayed in red. You may need to look at ways to shift your consumption to save money.
