This data can be up to 48 hours behind and does not include solar export credits.

^^ Graphs show the cost of your usage during each of the Midday Saver Pilot time bands for the period selected (left chart) and where data is available, for the same period 12 months earlier (right chart). The costs are usage or consumption only multiplied by the unit rate and do not include daily supply charge, concession credits and solar export credits.

Electricity metering consumption data is provided to Synergy by Western Power, typically within 2 Business days of collection from the meter.

Consumption data displayed in the graphs above is based on actual usage. If data is unavailable for any graph or any time period, the respective graph will not display any data.
