Synergy is working to help you with your energy usage through the addition of comparisons with others and energy saving tips.

By filling out your Household Profile along with the type of appliances you have in your home, we will provide more accurate comparisons with other households like yours as well as energy saving tips that are more relevant to you.

To begin, we first need you to fill out your basic profile, and add your appliances. It will take about 5 minutes to complete.
