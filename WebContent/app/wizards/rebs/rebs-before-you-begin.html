Before you begin

Apply here to participate in the Distributed Energy Buyback Scheme (DEBS), to install a new distributed energy system, or to upgrade your existing system size and/pr inverter capacity.

Ensure you've got a copy of your energy bill to complete this process.

If you have moved into a property which has an existing distributed energy system please call 13 13 53 for Residential or 13 13 54 for Business.
