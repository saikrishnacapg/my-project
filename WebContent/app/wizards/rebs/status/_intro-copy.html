Before you begin

Please do not refresh your browser or use the back or forward buttons of your browser while completing this process.

Apply here to

  • participate in the Distributed Energy Buyback Scheme (DEBS)
  • to install a new distributed energy system
  • upgrade your existing system size and/or inverter capacity

Before you begin your online application please ensure that you know if you are applying to install a new distributed energy system, or if you are requesting to upgrade your existing system size and/or inverter capacity.

If you have moved into a property which has an existing distributed energy system please call us on 13 13 53.

You can read more about distributed energy systems and their benefits here.

If you have a battery storage system or if you are participating in the Energy Storage Trial at Alkimos Beach please complete and return a Distributed energy system and/or battery storage system application form.
